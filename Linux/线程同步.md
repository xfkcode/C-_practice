             1.定义互斥锁变量 `pthread_mutex_t mutex;`  
		     2.在main函数中初始化互斥锁 `pthread_mutex_init(&mutex, NULL);`  
    	     3.在共享资源操作上下加锁/解锁   
		         	`pthread_mutex_lock(&mutex);` 
                  	//共享资源操作代码//  
                 	`pthread_mutex_unlock(&mutex);`  
        	 4.在main函数中销毁互斥锁 `pthread_mutex_destroy(&mutex);`
